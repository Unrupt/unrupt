## Release Notes

- Allow the video to toggle on and off without refreshing the page.
- Allow users to turn off their video and still receive the other user video stream.
- Fixed the ending of unrupt call when the screen times out.
- Improved the look and feel of the webapp
- Changed buttons from image based icons to font awesome icons.
- Added highlighter to indicate when someone is speaking.

## Known Issues with Unrupt and WebRTC

- User has to grant permissions for their webcam and microphone. This user gesture is required by major browsers, which might interrupt the process of the application. 
- On iPhone 7 and above, the buffering becomes unstable after clicking on mute then unmute two times (Unrupt only)
